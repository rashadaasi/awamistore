FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to our most common questions

Do you offer home delivery?
Yes! We offer same-day home delivery within the city. Orders above Rs. 500 qualify for free delivery. For outskirts, a small delivery charge applies.
How can I place an order via WhatsApp?
Simply send us a message on 0321-9876543 with your product list, address, and preferred delivery time. We'll confirm your order within minutes.
What is your return policy?
We offer a 7-day hassle-free return on all eligible products. Items must be unused and in original packaging. Perishables are non-returnable.
Are your products 100% original?
Absolutely. We source directly from authorized distributors and brand-certified suppliers. All products come with original manufacturer seals.
Do you offer bulk or wholesale pricing?
Yes, we have special wholesale pricing for businesses, schools, and large households. Contact us at 0311-1234567 or visit the store for bulk pricing.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept cash on delivery (COD), JazzCash, Easypaisa, bank transfer, and debit/credit cards. Online orders use secure payment gateways.
